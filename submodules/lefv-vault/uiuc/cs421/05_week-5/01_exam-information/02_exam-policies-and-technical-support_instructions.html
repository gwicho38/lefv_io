Exam Policies

The following polices will be enforced while taking the exam:

  • The exam will be proctored via ProctorU.

  • The exam is timed for 2 hours under ProctorU's supervision.

  • The exam only allows 1 attempt .

  • Make sure you sign up for an account via ProctorU. To sign up for an account, please go to: https://go.proctoru.com/students/users/new?institution=12 .

  • You need to schedule the exam 72 hours prior to the exam start window . After that, you must schedule by phone with an additional premium rush fee.

  • Proctored exams require stable internet connection, webcam, and microphone. It is strongly recommended that you pass the ProctorU System Test .

  • Please ensure you are connected to an Internet connection for the entire duration of exam. Otherwise, you will have to go through a lot of red tape.

  • If you are disconnected from the proctor during the exam for more than 5 minutes , you must reschedule the exam with ProctorU (for free) in a timely fashion within the exam two-day window. So it is highly recommended that you pass the ProctorU System Test before you take the exam. An incident reported will be generated and sent to your instructor if you disconnect from the proctor for over 5 minutes.

Permitted Resources During the Exam

  • Scratch Paper

  • You can test your files locally

  • No other resources are allowed (cheat sheets, books, programming language grade students)

For Live Technical Support

If you run into technical issues accessing the exam before taking it, you can contact Coursera Live Chat for assistance. If you encounter any issue with the exam while taking the exam , the proctor will seek assistance on your behalf.

To access Coursera Live Chat:

  1. Go to Coursera Learner Center: https://learner.coursera.help .

  2. Make sure to log in to your Coursera account.

  3. On the bottom right of the page, you will be prompted with a chat box that connects you to a Coursera live assistant.

  4. After you are connected with the live chat agent, make sure that you mention to the agent that you are an registered student in the MCS-DS program from University of Illinois.
