Week 10-12: Team Project & Readings on DC vs ML

Overview

In the remaining weeks, you are working with your group on the final team project, so there are no new lectures and assignments in these final weeks. The readings below provide an opportunity to look beyond the classic data cleaning approaches and learn about how data cleaning (DC) and machine learning (ML) may be related and interdependent. For example, the first reading asks the question: Is DC used to help with ML use cases or vice versa: do we use ML to help DC?

Weekly Readings

  • [IR22] Ilyas, I. F. & Rekatsinas, T. Machine Learning and Data Cleaning: Which Serves the Other ? J. Data and Information Quality 14, 13:1-13:11 (2022).

  • [LSG20] Lattar, Hafsa, Aicha Ben Salem, and Henda Hajjami Ben Ghezala. Does data cleaning improve heart disease prediction? . Procedia Computer Science 176 (2020): 1131-1140.

  • [LLB+21] Lee, Ga Young, Lubna Alzamil, Bakhtiyar Doskenov, and Arash Termehchy. A survey on data cleaning methods for improved machine learning model performance. arXiv preprint arXiv:2109.07127 (2021).
