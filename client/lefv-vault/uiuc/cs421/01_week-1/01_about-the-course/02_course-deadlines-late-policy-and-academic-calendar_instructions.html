Late Policy

  • Unless otherwise specified, all assignments are due at 11:59 PM Central Time. ( Time Zone Converter )

  • Late MPs will be accepted up to one week after the deadline, but the score will be capped at 80%.

  • No assignment will be accepted after the last day of class.

  • You can also see when each assignment is due by clicking the Assignments tab on the left menu bar.
